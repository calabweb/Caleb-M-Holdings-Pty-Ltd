MEET THE OWNER

Owner and lead planner, Calab Matoma Maboya, is passionate about lifestyle, rural living and executing exciting events for his clients. Born and raised in Moletjie Ga-chokoe,Polokwane, Limpopo, Calab has been planning corporate events full time since 2018. Calab studied National Diploma in Office Management and Technology at the Tshwane University of Technology, in Polokwane where his passion for business grow and he was exposed to the event planning industry, management skills, leadership skills,where it helps him to relate with their fellow co-workers and know how to deal well with his subordinates, which allows him the easy flow of activities in the company. Some events Calab has managed and executed since opening Caleb M Holdings (Pty) Ltd in 2021 are:Graduation ceremonies, Family Gatherings, wedding Celebrations,Parties, School Learners Awards ceremonies, Church Services, and Community Meetings Gatherings . Calab has also completed a Business course at NEMISA NPC where he was learning the adaptability to digital and technology to business world, to develop digital skills on how to explore, run and grow a digital enterprise. Passionate about Event Management business and getting involved with the community, Calab is an active supporter of local events, not-for-profit groups, festivals and the farmers market.
